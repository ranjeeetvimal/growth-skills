# Growth Tactics Library — Reference

## Viral Loops
1. **Referral Loop**: Give X, Get X (Dropbox, Uber)
2. **Collaboration Loop**: Team invites (Slack, Notion)
3. **Content Loop**: UGC sharing (Canva, Loom)
4. **Usage Loop**: Milestone shares (Grammarly, Strava)
5. **Integration Loop**: Workflow embedding (Zapier, Stripe)
6. **Network Loop**: Two-sided value (Airbnb, LinkedIn)

## Onboarding Tactics
1. **Progressive Disclosure**: Show features as needed
2. **Template Gallery**: Pre-built templates reduce TTV
3. **Forced Aha Moment**: Make core action unskippable
4. **Checklist Gamification**: Progress bars + rewards
5. **Empty State Design**: Never blank screens
6. **Human Touch**: Founder onboarding for first 100

## Activation Tactics
1. **Celebration Moments**: Confetti at milestones
2. **Progress Visualization**: "2 steps away from..."
3. **Social Proof Injection**: "847 people did X this week"
4. **Contextual Tips**: Show tips at the right moment

## Retention Tactics
1. **Streak Mechanics**: Consecutive day counters
2. **Weekly Stats Email**: "This week you..."
3. **Re-engagement Sequences**: Day 3, 7, 30 win-back
4. **Feature Adoption Nudges**: "Try this feature..."
5. **Community Building**: Private Discord/Slack

## Monetization Tactics
1. **Usage-Based Triggers**: Offer upgrade at 80% limit
2. **Feature Teasers**: Grayed-out Pro features
3. **Annual Prepay**: 20-30% discount
4. **Founding Member Pricing**: Lock in low price
5. **Risk Reversal**: Money-back guarantee

## Content Tactics
1. **SEO Comparisons**: "[You] vs [Competitor]"
2. **Problem-Aware SEO**: "How to [solve problem]"
3. **Product-Led Content**: Interactive calculators
4. **Building in Public**: Transparent journey sharing
5. **Template Libraries**: Free resources as lead gen
6. **Video Series**: Recurring format builds audience

## Channel Tactics
1. **ASO**: App Store optimization
2. **Reddit Marketing**: Genuine value first
3. **HN/PH Launch**: Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT
4. **Newsletter Sponsorships**: Sponsor ICP newsletters
5. **Integration Partnerships**: Build + co-market
6. **Open-Source Marketing**: OSS parts of product
