# Content Playbook

## Channel concentration
Pick 1-2 channels by evidence of where the ICP already is. Ignore the rest for 90
days. "We should be everywhere" is how pre-PMF teams do nothing well.

## SEO cluster patterns (priority order)
1. **Switcher intent** — "[incumbent] alternatives", "[incumbent] pricing". Highest
   urgency but a DECAYING asset (spikes when the incumbent stumbles, fades after).
2. **Fear / risk intent** — "is X safe/allowed", "will I get [bad outcome]". Low
   competition, COMPOUNDS, and competitors end up citing your pages. The durable moat.
3. **Job-to-be-done** — "how to [do the core job]", "[category] for [persona]".
4. **Programmatic long-tail** — one indexable page per template × per niche.

## Comparison pages (do them well)
Honest feature matrix + "which is right for you" framework + a live demo CTA. Also
build competitor-vs-competitor pages to catch undecided searchers on neutral ground.
Only do SEO if the ICP actually searches, you can rank in ~6 months, and you can
sustain the cadence. Otherwise skip it and do community/content loops.

## Engineering-as-marketing (free tools)
Ship a tool your ICP shares: a cost/"tax" calculator vs an incumbent, a risk/health
checker, a generator for their recurring task. Built for links + shares. Ship the
timely one (e.g. a switcher calculator) while the window is hot.

## Demo-as-funnel
End every asset with a live trigger/demo, not just a link — the product demonstrates
itself and captures the lead in one move.

## 90-day calendar (cadence, not a backlog)
Columns: SEO (2/wk) · Video/short-form (3/wk) · Community/other. Sequence:
weeks 1-2 capture the timely intent + ship the free tool; 3-4 comparison pages +
partner outreach; 5-6 pillar pages + first case study; 7-8 how-to + launch prep;
9-10 persona pages + public launch (Product Hunt / Show HN); 11-12 programmatic
batch + building-in-public.

## Distribution
Repurposing chain: pillar → short-form clips → carousel → newsletter → genuine
community answers (value first, link only where organic). One disclosed brand
account, no astroturfing.

## Content bet format
Channel + format, why (evidence), expected outcome (specific metric), confidence,
effort/week, risk, 30-day read. Name 3-5 channels you're NOT doing and why.
